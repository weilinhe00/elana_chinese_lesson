grammar 1
🛒 购物句型 gòuwù jùxíng · shopping sentence patterns
怎么买东西 zěnme mǎi dōngxi
how to buy things — useful sentence patterns
我要买 ___ 。
wǒ yào mǎi ___ .
I want to buy ___ .
我要买一些水果。 yīxiē shuǐguǒ
I want to buy some fruit.
我想买 ___ 。
wǒ xiǎng mǎi ___ .
I'd like to buy ___ .
我想买一个熊玩偶！ xióng wán'ǒu
I want to buy a teddy bear!
请给我 ___ 。
qǐng gěi wǒ ___ .
Please give me ___ .
请给我两瓶牛奶。 liǎng píng niúnǎi
Please give me 2 bottles of milk.
___ 在哪里？
___ zài nǎli?
Where is ___ ?
玩偶在哪里？ wán'ǒu
Where are the plushies?
我看看 ___ 。
wǒ kàn kàn ___ .
Let me look at ___ .
我看看那个熊猫玩偶。 nàge xióngmāo
Let me look at that panda plushie.
一共 ___ 块。
yīgòng ___ kuài .
Total: ___ kuai.
一共五十块。 wǔshí kuài
Total: 50 kuai.

grammar 3
🔑 关键动词 guānjiàn dòngcí · key verbs for shopping
购物的动词 gòuwù de dòngcí
verbs you'll use at the supermarket
买 mǎi · to buy
我要买苹果。
wǒ yào mǎi píngguǒ.
I want to buy apples.
卖 mài · to sell
这里卖玩偶吗？
zhèlǐ mài wán'ǒu ma?
Do you sell plushies here?
想 xiǎng · would like to
我想买一个熊猫玩偶。
wǒ xiǎng mǎi yī gè xióngmāo wán'ǒu.
I'd like to buy a panda plushie.
找 zhǎo · look for / give change
找你五块。
zhǎo nǐ wǔ kuài.
Here's 5 kuai change.
付钱 fù qián · to pay
我用手机付钱。 wǒ yòng shǒujī fù qián. I'll pay with my phone.

对话 2
💬 对话 (二) · asking where things are 问路
场景二：在哪里？chǎngjǐng èr
scene 2: where is it? — asking directions
🤔
Elana
请问，水果在哪里？
qǐng wèn, shuǐguǒ zài nǎli?
Excuse me, where are the fruits?
🙋‍♀️
服务员 fúwùyuán · staff
水果在那边。
shuǐguǒ zài nàbiān.
The fruits are over there.
😊
Elana
谢谢！那玩偶呢？
xièxie! nà wán'ǒu ne?
Thanks! What about plushies?
🙋‍♀️
服务员
玩偶在二楼。
wán'ǒu zài èr lóu.
Plushies are on the second floor.
🎉
Elana
太好了！谢谢您！
tài hǎo le! xièxie nín!
Great! Thank you!
🔑 关键词
那边 nàbiān = over there · 那 ___ 呢? nà ___ ne? = what about ___ ? · 二楼 èr lóu = 2nd floor
