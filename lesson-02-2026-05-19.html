vocabulary 7
🥢 量词 liàngcí · measure words — VERY important!
量词 liàngcí — counting words
In Chinese, we put a special word between a number and a thing. 一 + 量词 + 名词 (yī + liàngcí + míngcí)
个
gè
general / for anything
一个汉堡 · 1 burger
杯
bēi
a cup of (drinks)
一杯水 · 1 cup water
碗
wǎn
a bowl of
一碗面条 · 1 bowl noodles
盘
pán
a plate of
一盘饺子 · 1 plate dumplings
份
fèn
a serving of
一份沙拉 · 1 salad
瓶
píng
a bottle of
一瓶可乐 · 1 bottle cola
⚠️ very important rule
English: "I want a burger" → Chinese: 我要 一个 汉堡 (yī gè hànbǎo)
You can't just say "一汉堡" — you must put 个 gè in between! 一定要量词！yīdìng yào liàngcí!
vocabulary 8
🥢 量词应用 liàngcí yìngyòng · measure words in action
量词 + 食物 — measure words with food
Which measure word goes with which food? 哪个量词配哪个食物？
🍚 碗 wǎn (bowl)
一碗米饭 yī wǎn mǐfàn · 1 bowl of rice
一碗面条 yī wǎn miàntiáo · 1 bowl of noodles
一碗汤 yī wǎn tāng · 1 bowl of soup
🍽️ 盘 pán (plate)
一盘饺子 yī pán jiǎozi · 1 plate dumplings
一盘炒饭 yī pán chǎofàn · 1 plate fried rice
一盘沙拉 yī pán shālā · 1 plate of salad
🥤 杯 bēi (cup)
一杯水 yī bēi shuǐ · 1 cup of water
两杯奶茶 liǎng bēi nǎichá · 2 cups milk tea
三杯果汁 sān bēi guǒzhī · 3 cups of juice
🍔 个 gè (any item)
一个汉堡 yī gè hànbǎo · 1 burger
两个包子 liǎng gè bāozi · 2 buns
一个鸡蛋 yī gè jīdàn · 1 egg
💡 special: 二 vs 两
Counting "two of something" → use 两 liǎng, not 二 èr. So 两碗面条 liǎng wǎn miàntiáo ✅  ·  二碗面条 ❌
grammar
🔑 关键动词 guānjiàn dòngcí · key verbs for ordering
点菜的动词 diǎn cài de dòngcí — verbs for ordering
5 important verbs you'll use again and again at the restaurant!
要 yào — to want
我要一碗面条 wǒ yào yī wǎn miàntiáo
I want a bowl of noodles
点 diǎn — to order (dishes)
我想点一个汉堡 wǒ xiǎng diǎn yī gè hànbǎo
I'd like to order a burger
来 lái — bring / I'll have
来一份饺子 lái yī fèn jiǎozi
I'll have a serving of dumplings (casual!)
给 gěi — to give
请给我菜单 qǐng gěi wǒ càidān
please give me the menu
请 qǐng — please / polite please-verb
请给我一杯水 qǐng gěi wǒ yī bēi shuǐ  ·  please give me a cup of water
请问菜单在哪里？ qǐng wèn càidān zài nǎli?  ·  excuse me, where is the menu?

几位？
jǐ wèi?
how many people?

liǎng wèi / sān wèi
2 people / 3 people
请给我菜单。
qǐng gěi wǒ càidān.
please give me the menu.
我要___。
wǒ yào ___ .
I want ___.
来一份___。
lái yī fèn ___ .
I'll have a serving of ___.
这是什么菜？
zhè shì shénme cài?
what dish is this?
不要辣，谢谢。
bú yào là, xièxie.
no spicy, thank you.
多一点 / 少一点
duō yīdiǎn / shǎo yīdiǎn
a little more / a little less

A or B?

duōshǎo qián?
how much money?
