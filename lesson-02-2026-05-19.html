⚠️ 重要规则 zhòngyào guīzé · very important rule
English: "I want a burger" → Chinese: 我要一个汉堡 wǒ yào yī gè hànbǎo
You can't say "一汉堡" — you MUST put 个 gè in between!
grammar 2
🥢 量词 + 食物 · measure words with food
量词应用 liàngcí yìngyòng
measure words in action — which one fits which food?
🍚 碗 wǎn · bowl
一碗米饭 yī wǎn mǐfàn 1 bowl of rice
一碗面条 yī wǎn miàntiáo 1 bowl of noodles
一碗汤 yī wǎn tāng 1 bowl of soup
🍽️ 盘 pán · plate
一盘饺子 yī pán jiǎozi 1 plate of dumplings
一盘炒饭 yī pán chǎofàn 1 plate of fried rice
一盘沙拉 yī pán shālā 1 plate of salad
🥤 杯 bēi · cup
一杯水 yī bēi shuǐ 1 cup of water
两杯奶茶 liǎng bēi nǎichá 2 cups of milk tea
三杯果汁 sān bēi guǒzhī 3 cups of juice
🍔 个 gè · item (general)
一个汉堡 yī gè hànbǎo 1 burger
两个包子 liǎng gè bāozi 2 buns
一个鸡蛋 yī gè jīdàn 1 egg
💡 特殊规则 tèshū guīzé · special: 二 vs 两
Counting "two of something" → use 两 liǎng, NOT 二 èr!
两碗面条 liǎng wǎn miàntiáo ✅ (2 bowls of noodles)  ·  二碗面条 ❌
grammar 3
🔑 关键动词 guānjiàn dòngcí · key verbs for ordering
点菜的动词 diǎn cài de dòngcí
5 important verbs you'll use again and again!
要 yào · to want
我要一碗面条。
wǒ yào yī wǎn miàntiáo.
I want a bowl of noodles.
点 diǎn · to order (food)
我想点一个汉堡。
wǒ xiǎng diǎn yī gè hànbǎo.
I'd like to order a burger.
来 lái · I'll have / bring
来一份饺子。
lái yī fèn jiǎozi.
I'll have a serving of dumplings (casual).
给 gěi · to give
请给我菜单。
qǐng gěi wǒ càidān.
Please give me the menu.
请 qǐng · please
请给我一杯水。 qǐng gěi wǒ yī bēi shuǐ. Please give me a cup of water.
请问菜单在哪里？ qǐng wèn càidān zài nǎli? Excuse me, where is the menu?
